Beef stew recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Raw beef
  • Salt pile
  • Carrot
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. To make a delicious beef stew, begin by searing cubes of beef in a large pot with oil.
  2. Add chopped carrots, and smashed garlic.
  3. Let it simmer over the fire until the meat is tender and the flavors meld together.
  4. Serve it with a strong Nord Mead.
