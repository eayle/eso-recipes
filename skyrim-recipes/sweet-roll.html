Sweet roll recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Jug of milk
  • Salt pile
  • Sack of flour
  • Butter
  • Chicken's egg

Instructions:

  1. To make a delightful sweet roll, first, whisk together the softened butter and milk.
  2. Gradually add flour and salt to form a dough.
  3. Roll it out, cut into circles, bake for 10-12 minutes.
